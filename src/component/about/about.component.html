Our Story

Founded in 2019, SARVAM NGO was born out of a passion for addressing social inequalities. With a focus on holistic development, we aim to provide educational opportunities, mental health support, and empower women to become leaders in their communities.

Vision

A world where every person has the opportunity to live with dignity, knowledge, and mental well-being.

Mission

To uplift communities through holistic programs providing child education, mental health resources, and women empowerment, fostering self-reliance and resilience.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Upholding transparency, accountability, and honesty in all that we do.
  • Empowerment: Providing individuals with the tools and resources to improve their lives.
  • Collaboration: Partnering with communities, volunteers, and donors to achieve sustainable outcomes.
  • Compassion: Approaching our work with empathy and a genuine commitment to social welfare.
